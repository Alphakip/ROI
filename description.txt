== Changelog ==
0.1.0 - The hunt for the void:
- Introducing the void class, a class massively based debuffing around your enemy and buffing your friends, click on it and watch the magic happen.
- Introducing the void affinity, collect void affinity by doing certain task and use it to craft rare relic that will help your in your journey.
- Introducing the void tier, a mechanic related to void affinity, the higher tier you are, the more danger you get, but the more reward you get.
- Introducing the void pillar, a boss that have a chance to spawn in the higher void tier, it is not related to the moon lord ;)
